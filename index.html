About giraffes

Giraffes are the world's tallest mammals and they are known for their long neck which is taller than the average man and weighs up to 600 pounds.
However, scientist still does not know why giraffes evolved to have so long necks.
All we can know is that giraffes have seven vertebrae or neck bone as us human do execpt their bones are extremly long.
Some characteristics that they have are...
Tallest mammal
Very long neck
Short, stiff mane
Long, black hair on tail; used as an insect-swatter
Long prehensile tongue: 50 cm (20 in)
Large brown eyes bordered by long, black lashes
Large hooved feet
