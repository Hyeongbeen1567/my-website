Giraffe kicks

Giraffes are the tallest living mammal with height of 16ft to 20ft (5m to 6m) and well known for its friendly personality.
So people tend to underestimate their power.
However, giraffes are very strong and fast.
A well-placed kick could shatter the skull of a predatory lion, and giraffes reach speeds more than 55km per hour.
Most of animal can not overpower giraffes alone.

Necking

Giraffes are usually very friendly and gentle. But in some circumstances, male giraffes fight for dominance and access to females.
However, they fight in a unique way called “necking”.
2 giraffes stand shoulder to shoulder facing each other and they swing their heavily armed head with their long legs.
The fight goes on until one giraffe loses their balance or when one of them breaks their neck.
